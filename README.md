## How I Think About AI Engineering

AI engineering is not just prompting. It is the engineering work required to make AI systems reliable, useful, measurable, and integrated into real workflows.

That means building around:

- Model selection and model behavior.
- Tool use and external system integration.
- Memory, retrieval, and context engineering.
- Evaluation, observability, and failure analysis.
- Human review, permissions, and safety boundaries.
- Product workflows that make AI useful to real users.
